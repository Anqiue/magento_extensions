Installation:

1 - Backup your web directory
2 - Download and unzip extension contents on your computer and navigate inside the extracted folder. 
3 - Using your FTP client upload app, js and skin directory to your store root. 
4 - Go to System->Configuration->Sales->Checkout and configure checkout options.
5 - Refresh cache

That's all, the installation is complete.
